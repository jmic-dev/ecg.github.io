History

History

Our church's journey began in 1885 when a small group of faithful believers gathered in a modest building to worship and serve the community. Over the decades, we've grown from a humble congregation to a vibrant family of faith, reaching thousands through worship, outreach, and missions.

Through wars, economic challenges, and cultural shifts, our commitment to sharing the Gospel has remained steadfast. We've built schools, supported orphanages, and sent missionaries around the world. Today, we continue that legacy with modern ministries while honoring our rich heritage.
