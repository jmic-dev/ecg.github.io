Community Center Build

New facility for youth programs and outreach events

Project Overview

The new Community Center will serve as a hub for youth programs, Bible study groups, skills training, and community outreach events. This facility will significantly expand our capacity to reach young people and families in the area.

Project Goals

  • Provide a safe, modern space for youth discipleship and mentorship
  • Host weekly community events and skill development workshops
  • Support local families through education and health awareness programs
  • Create a central location for church outreach activities

Current Status

Construction is 40% complete. We have successfully completed the foundation and main structure. The next phase includes roofing, electrical work, and interior finishing.
