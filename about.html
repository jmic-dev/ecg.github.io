About Us

A community of faith, love, and service

Who We Are

The Evangelical Church of The Gambia (ECG) is a vibrant, Bible-believing church committed to worshiping God, making disciples, and serving our communities with the love of Christ.

Our Mission

To glorify God by proclaiming the Gospel, nurturing believers, and transforming communities through the power of the Holy Spirit.

Our Vision

To be a Spirit-filled church that reaches every corner of The Gambia and beyond with the saving knowledge of Jesus Christ.

Our Story

Founded with a passion for genuine worship and biblical teaching, ECG has grown from a small fellowship into a network of churches and preaching points across the country. Through the years, God has faithfully guided us in ministry, outreach, education, and community development.
